For Your Meeting

What to Discuss With Your Advisor

These are the must-know topics for your next conversation, in priority order.

  • 1

    Concentration risk with wealth tied predominantly to Ellison Dynamics

    Nearly half of your $187 million net worth is tied to a single business, creating significant illiquidity and sector-specific risk. A successful exit will provide an opportunity to diversify your holdings and build a more resilient financial position for the long term.

  • 2

    QSBS eligibility preservation and deal structure for a tax-efficient exit

    Your shares appear to qualify for the Section 1202 QSBS exclusion, but deal structure matters. Ensuring the transaction is structured as a stock sale — and that eligibility is preserved through closing — needs to be addressed before any sale process begins.

  • 3

    Federal estate tax exposure on post-sale assets without proper wealth transfer planning

    With a taxable estate of approximately $178 million, post-sale liquidity will face substantial federal estate tax exposure without proactive planning. Early exploration of GRATs, IDGTs, and other transfer strategies is essential to protect the wealth you are working to release.

  • 4

    Philanthropic planning through a donor-advised fund post-exit

    You and your wife are considering a donor-advised fund as a vehicle for charitable giving after the exit. This approach offers flexibility and lower administrative overhead compared to a private foundation, while allowing meaningful impact over time.

  • 5

    Emotional transition and identity after stepping away from the business

    After nearly a decade of building Ellison Dynamics, stepping away will bring emotional and identity challenges beyond the financial numbers. Planning that addresses purpose, engagement, and lifestyle will be an important part of the conversation.
